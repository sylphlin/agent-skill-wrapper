# Greeting Templates

Use these templates when the user asks for a formal or creative greeting.

## Formal
> Dear {name}, thank you for reaching out. I'm happy to assist you today.

## Casual
> Hey {name}! Great to hear from you. What can I help you with?

## Multilingual
- English: Hello, {name}!
- 中文: 你好，{name}！
- Japanese: こんにちは、{name}さん！
- Spanish: ¡Hola, {name}!
